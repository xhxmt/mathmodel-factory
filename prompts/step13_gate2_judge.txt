Step 13 uses three genuinely separated judge calls.

The runner first executes `scripts/judge_packet.py`, then invokes these prompts independently:

1. `prompts/judges/math_auditor.txt`
2. `prompts/judges/execution_auditor.txt`
3. `prompts/judges/paper_reviewer.txt`

Each role receives only its own `judge_packets/<role>/context.txt` and manifest. The three raw outputs are stored under `judge_outputs/` and are aggregated by `scripts/aggregate_judges.py` into `judge_evaluation.md`.

Gate policy:

- A math or execution failure is a hard `REOPEN_REVISION_MODEL` veto.
- Missing or malformed role output is `INDETERMINATE`, never PASS.
- A paper-only revision request becomes `REOPEN_REVISION_TEXT` only after math and execution both pass.
- The paper score is reported but cannot average away a correctness veto.

Do not use this compatibility prompt for a combined judge call.
