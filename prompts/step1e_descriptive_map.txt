WHEN FINISHED, write descriptive_map.md — a comprehensive report organized as follows:

## Data Overview
Dataset dimensions, unit of observation, panel structure, time coverage, key variables and their types.

## Variable-by-Variable Summary

## Key Bivariate Patterns
The most important correlations and conditional associations, organized thematically. Which variables are strongly associated? Which associations are surprisingly weak or absent? Which relationships are nonlinear?

## Patterns Most Relevant to the Research Question

## Catalog of All Outputs
A numbered list of every figure and table produced, with a one-line description of each.
